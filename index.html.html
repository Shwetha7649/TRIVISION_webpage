TriVision: VIT-RIT-Industry AI & Data Science Summit 2025

"Driving Innovation and Collaboration for a Sustainable Future"

7th - 9th January 2025

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) and Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT), New York, in collaboration with industry partners, are organizing the TriVision'25 -AI and Data Science Summit focusing on significant advancements in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. It aims to emphasize the vital role of industry-academia collaborations in driving innovation and impactful applications.
